## App Description

### English

**Fred - The Quiet Time Challenge**

Turn quiet time into a fun game! Fred helps kids learn to stay quiet by making it a challenge they can win.

**How It Works:**
Set a timer and watch the colorful sound meter. If the noise stays low, kids earn a spectacular confetti celebration when time runs out. But if it gets too loud? The timer resets and they start over!

**Perfect For:**
• Quiet time at home
• Homework sessions
• Reading time
• Calming down before bed
• Car rides
• Waiting rooms
• Teaching self-regulation

**Features:**
• Real-time noise monitoring with visual feedback
• Colorful sound meter that changes from yellow to coral to fuchsia as volume increases
• Gentle vibration warnings before reset
• Stunning confetti physics celebration on success
• Customizable timer (1-60 minutes)
• Adjustable noise thresholds
• Simple, kid-friendly interface
• Works offline - no internet required
• No ads, no tracking, no in-app purchases

**Why Fred?**
Fred makes the challenge visible and rewarding. Kids can see exactly how loud they are and feel motivated to keep that meter in the green zone. When they succeed, they're rewarded with a delightful animation that makes quiet time feel like an achievement, not a punishment.

Fred uses your device's microphone to measure ambient noise levels. All processing happens on your device - nothing is recorded or transmitted.

## App Review Notes
*Private notes for App Review team*

**Test Instructions:**

1. Grant microphone permission when prompted
2. Tap "Start" to begin timer
3. Make noise (speak, clap) to see the sound meter react
4. Notice the meter changes from yellow → coral → fuchsia as volume increases
5. If volume exceeds 90dB, timer will reset (test by playing loud music or shouting)
6. To quickly test completion: Use the "DEBUG: Skip to end" button (only visible in development builds)
7. On completion, enjoy the confetti celebration

**Microphone Permission:**
The app requires microphone access to monitor ambient noise levels. This is core to the app's functionality - teaching kids to maintain quiet behavior through visual feedback and gamification.
