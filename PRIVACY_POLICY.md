# Privacy Policy for Fred Timer

**Last Updated:** March 17, 2026

## Overview

Fred Timer ("Fred", "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

## Data Collection

**We collect NO personal data.** Specifically:

- **No account required:** Fred does not require user registration or accounts
- **No analytics:** Fred does not use analytics or tracking services
- **No advertising:** Fred contains no advertisements or ad tracking
- **No third-party services:** Fred does not connect to any external servers or services
- **No personal information:** Fred does not collect names, emails, or any identifying information

## Microphone Usage

Fred requires microphone access to measure ambient noise levels for its core functionality (monitoring sound to reset the timer when noise exceeds thresholds).

**Important:**
- Audio is processed in real-time on your device only
- NO audio is recorded, saved, or transmitted
- Audio data is never stored permanently
- Audio data is never sent to external servers
- The microphone is only active when the timer is running

## Local Data Storage

Fred stores only the following settings locally on your device:
- Timer duration preference (1-60 minutes)
- Noise threshold settings (warning and reset levels)
- Language preference (English/Norwegian)

This data:
- Remains on your device only
- Is not transmitted anywhere
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## Children's Privacy

Fred is designed for families with children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. The app requires no data input beyond basic settings adjustments.

## Data Security

Since Fred collects no personal data and operates entirely offline, there is no data to secure or transmit. All processing occurs locally on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last Updated" date.
